üåº Treatment and Care

Mood disorders are highly treatable with the right combination of therapy, medication, and lifestyle adjustments.

  • Therapy: Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) helps identify and change negative thought patterns.
  • Medication: Antidepressants or mood stabilizers prescribed by a psychiatrist.
  • Lifestyle: Regular exercise, sufficient sleep, balanced diet, and stress management.
  • Support: Joining mental health support groups or counseling sessions for emotional connection.
